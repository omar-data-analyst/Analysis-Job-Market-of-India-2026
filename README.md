# Analysis-Job-Market-of-India-2026
- Analysis of 10,000 job applications describing the highest and lowest salaries offered, the nature of the work (remote, hybrid, and on-site), required years of experience, required skills, the hiring company, and the geographical location.

**The main objectives of the analysis were:**

1- My analysis aimed to reveal the impact of each of the above factors on the average salary offered.

2- To identify the preferred demographics and extract the most sought-after skills by companies.

3- To understand the general policies implemented by companies in the Indian labor market.
